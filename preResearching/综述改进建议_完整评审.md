## 3) 逐段/逐区块改进建议（按文章结构）

## A. 题目、作者与摘要区

- **问题**：作者与摘要仍是模板示例文本，不对应论文内容。
- **建议**：统一替换为真实作者与内容导向摘要；摘要建议采用 5 句式：背景→缺口→方法→贡献→结论。

## B. CCS / Keywords / metadata

- **问题**：CCS 与关键词是 “Do Not Use …” 占位语。
- **建议**：CCS 选择应覆盖 `Robotics`、`Machine learning`、`Computer vision`、`Human-centered computing`（按目标 venue 微调）；关键词控制在 6–8 个。

## C. Introduction

- **优点**：问题意识清楚，贡献声明完整，且有“系统视角”主线。
- **可加强点**：
  1) 加一个“本文与已有综述的差异表（3–5列）”；
  2) 增加定量趋势（例如近三年数据规模、跨本体数据比例）；
  3) 在“Scope”加入明确时间边界。

## D. System Architecture

- **优点**：硬件—中间件—控制分层非常清晰。
- **可加强点**：
  1) 把“平台示例”与“任务适配边界”显式对应（如装配/抓取/移动操控）；
  2) 增加“失效模式（latency jitter / calibration drift / contact instability）”小节。

## E. Data Ecosystem

- **优点**：K1/K2/K3 分层有解释力，表格信息量高。
- **可加强点**：
  1) 增加“数据偏差维度”分析（场景偏置、语言模板偏置、成功样本偏置）；
  2) 对真实与仿真数据给出“用途边界图”（pretrain / finetune / eval）；
  3) 建议新增“失败轨迹如何进入训练目标”的统一叙述。

## F. Learning Paradigms（若干节）

- **建议重点**：将 Type I/II/III 的取舍写成“可决策表”——输入条件（数据规模、实时性、算力）→推荐范式。

## G. Evaluation

- **优点**：已覆盖性能、泛化、能效等多维度。
- **可加强点**：
  1) 增加最小评估协议（minimum reporting standard）；
  2) 明确“可复现要求”字段（种子、硬件、频率、失败定义）；
  3) 给出跨论文可比的标准化指标模板。

## H. Challenges & Conclusion

- **优点**：提出跨接口瓶颈（representation/deployment/ecosystem）。
- **可加强点**：每条挑战后面配“近中期里程碑”与“可验证实验路径”。

## 4) 可直接替换的摘要（英文版，面向 ACM 风格）

> Embodied manipulation has recently advanced through vision-language-action models, world models, and large-scale robot data, yet progress remains fragmented across hardware, data pipelines, learning architectures, and deployment constraints. This survey presents a systems-oriented synthesis of embodied AI for robotic arms, organized along the chain of embodiment, data ecosystem, learning paradigms, and evaluation. We first analyze how end-effector morphology, platform mobility, and middleware/control stacks shape feasible action spaces and real-time reliability. We then propose a three-tier data taxonomy (skill-level, task-level, foundation-level) and review acquisition, simulation, automatic generation, and refinement pipelines that govern data scalability and transferability. Next, we unify cognitive planning, policy learning, and integration patterns into a coupled framework that clarifies trade-offs between semantic generalization, physical precision, and deployment cost. Finally, we summarize evaluation protocols across task performance, data efficiency, robustness, human preference alignment, and energy/system efficiency, and distill open challenges in representation, safety-critical deployment, and ecosystem standardization. The central takeaway is that future gains will depend less on isolated model improvements and more on tighter co-design across embodiment, data, learning, and runtime operation.

## 5) 可直接替换的结论（英文版，压缩有力）

> This survey reframed embodied manipulation for robotic arms as an end-to-end systems problem rather than a model-only problem. By connecting embodiment constraints, data production pipelines, learning architectures, and evaluation/deployment practice, we showed that the main bottlenecks emerge at interfaces between stages: perception-planning alignment, training-deployment safety transfer, and cross-platform standardization. Our synthesis suggests three concrete priorities for the field: (1) protocolized and failure-aware data infrastructures, (2) integration architectures that explicitly trade off generality, precision, and latency, and (3) evaluation standards that jointly measure capability, robustness, and operational efficiency. In short, scalable embodied intelligence will be achieved through cross-layer co-design across hardware, software, data, and learning—not by scaling any single component alone.
